As with many neighborhoods in Korea, Bullo Dong has many places to eat, most of them being meat or fried chicken restaurants. However, Bullo Dong also has a duck restaurant called Bullo Duck as well as a Lotteria that make Bullo Dong stand out from the surrounding neighborhoods.
