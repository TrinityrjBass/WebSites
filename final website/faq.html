Answers

Where is Bullo Dong?

 Bullo Dong is located just west of Gimpo (Kimpo) and north west of Seoul.
Map

What schools are near Bullo Dong?

 Bullo Dong has a number of schools ranging from day-care to middle school. There is also a high school that is 5 a minute bus ride away.

What about public transportation into Seoul?

 There are 2 red buses that go into Seoul from Bullo Dong, the 9501 and 1101 that go to Gangnam and Hapjeong respectively. The 60 also goes to Seoul going through Seongjeong (line 5), Yeomchang (line 9) and turns around at Dangsan (line 2)

Is there a Homeplus or Emart nearby>

 YES! There is a Homeplus that is a 5 minute bus (any non-red bus) ride away. The Emart is farther away, about 25-30 minutes by bus (903 or 17).

Where is the closest CostCo?

 The closest Costco is in Ilsan, about an hour by bus and 30 minutes by car/taxi.

Are there any 'foreigner' hangouts nearby?

 There are a number of English teachers in Gimpo (Kimpo) Sau-dong (20 minutes by bus 60 or 6641) and there are bars there whose staff speaks English.
