Welcome to Cooking in Korea, a guide to help you find what you need to make Western food during your stay in Korea.

Cooking Western food in Korea is a challenge. Finding the right ingredients (or ingredients that are close enough) and suitable equipment can be daunting, especially when first arriving in Korea. Sometimes you have to be a little creative to find solutions and know where to look.

This website will help you identify stores and what they contain, where to find good equipment, identify food items as well as provide helpful conversions and substitutions for cooking and baking. I hope that this website will help you make your favorite food while in Korea.
