Hello, my name is Trinity Bass. Both my wife and I love to cook. When we moved to Korea 5 years ago, we had to eat-out a lot because we didn't know where and what to look for in the grocery stores to make dishes that we grew up with. The biggest thing that helped us was that my wife had taken Korean in college. So there were some familiar things that we could find...like ketchup.

I hope that this web site helps you find what you are looking for, or at least something close enough so that you can make the food you miss.

For a glimpse at our first couple of years in Korea check out my wife's blog: kitchen in a fishbowl.
