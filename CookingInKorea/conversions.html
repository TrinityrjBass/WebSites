Temperature

First: some important tips that brear repeating:

  1. Don't leave the kitchen when you are cooking/baking something.
  2. Elevation effects leavening agents, it will take more the closer to sea-level you are.
  3. Convection ovens cook faster than conventional ovens
  4. If using a convection oven, the food may need to be rotated ~half way through cooking.

Substitutions

What really helps find good substitutions is knowing what the missing ingredient does in your recipe. When I look at a recipe, look for why each ingredient is listed, what role is it playing in the recipe. Vegetables are vegetables, meat is meat, but it is all still content. Don't have carrots? Use another vegetable, it'll be ok. That being said, if you don't know the role of each ingredient, here is a list of substitutions. The items on this table can be substituted both ways. For example, sourcream can be a substitute for yogurt and yogurt can be substituted for sour cream.

yogurt sourcream
corn starch potato starch
butter margarine
